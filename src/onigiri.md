# Onigiri

This recipe makes a 8 good sized onigiri.

### Bakeware

* rice paddle
* large bowl
* small bowl
* strainer
* heavy bottom pot

### Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
| -------- | ---------- |
| 310g | short-grain rice (e.g. arborio) |
| 350g | water |
| 72g or 4 tablespoon | rice vinegar |
| 45g or 3 tablespoon | sugar |
| 4g or 1 teaspoon | salt |
| 1 can | tuna
| 3 tablespoons | japanese mayo |
| 1 teaspoon | soy sauce |

### Instructions
* sieve rice over bowl and rinse with cold water
* remove excess startch
* when water runs clear, drain
* rice to heavy bottom pot with water
* soak for 30 min
* turn heat to high to boil
* turn to low for 15 minutes
* once no liquid remains, turn off heat and steam 10 minutes
* combine vinegar, sugar, salt in bowl
* dump rice into bowl
* cool rapidly by spreading rice
* fold rice with cutting motion and folding motion while cold blow dry rice
* ready when grains are shiny 
* put rice in mould, then tuna, then more rice
* press mould against counter gently to firm up
