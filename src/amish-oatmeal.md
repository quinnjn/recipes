# Amish Oatmeal

Enjoy with warm milk, fruit, or brown sugar

### Bakeware

* 9in x 13in baking pan
* Large mixing bowl

### Baking

350ºF / 176ºC for 25-30 minutes

### Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
| -------- | ---------- |
| 3 cups | quick cooking oats |
| 1 cup  | sugar |
| 1 cup  | milk |
| ½ cup  | butter, melted |
| 2 | eggs |
| 1 teaspoon | baking powder |
| 1½ teaspoon | salt | 
| 2 teaspoon | vanilla |

### Instructions

* Large mixing bowl
  * Mix ingredients
* 9x13 pan
  * Grease
  * Spread mixture
